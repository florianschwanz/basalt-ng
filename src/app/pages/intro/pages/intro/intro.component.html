This is Basalt
Basalt is a tool that helps you
  • keep track of and manage the work you have done
  • plan ahead and manage your To Dos
  • manage contacts
making it not only a to-do- but also a have-done-list
Define tasks
Tasks are the things you want to accomplish and allow you to define
  • a description
  • a priority
  • a due date
  • an associated project
  • a definition of done
  • etc.
Tasks are automatically sorted based on their
  • due date & due time
  • the estimated effort
  • and their priority
Therefore, if you maintain the necessary information, Basalt automatically tells you what's up next!
Make use of tasklets
A tasklet is a piece of work which can be associated with a task. Tasklets can be of different types such as
  • Simple Actions
  • Calls
  • Meetings
  • Checklists
All your tasklets will be displayed in the timeline. The work you've done will be automatically time-tracked.
Store your data securely
Basalt makes use of IndexedDB - a database which is part of your browser. No data is transferred to the cloud. Everything stays securely on your device.

We will be adding multi-device synchronization in a later release. If you wish to synchronize your data across multiple devices, there's an export and import feature you can use for now.
